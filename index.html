About Harmonee Wellness

Harmonee Wellness was created by a licensed mental health clinician, DEIB leader, group facilitator, and Leadership Psychology doctoral student with a deep commitment to trauma-informed, culturally aware, and emotionally responsible care.

Rooted in clinical experience, community-based healing, and reflective adult development, Harmonee blends therapy-informed education with nervous system awareness, emotional regulation, attachment work, identity-conscious reflection, and practical self-care tools for real life.

Harmonee Wellness recognizes that healing does not happen in isolation. Our patterns, relationships, identities, communities, and lived experiences all shape how we make meaning, protect ourselves, connect with others, and grow over time.

Through guided reflection tools, workbooks, self-paced pathways, and learning spaces, Harmonee supports people in building mental health hygiene that is compassionate, grounded, accessible, and deeply human.

Mental Health Hygiene Framework

The Harmonee Mental Health Hygiene Framework

Mental health hygiene is the daily practice of caring for your emotional, relational, and nervous system wellness before life becomes a crisis.

01

Pause

Notice what is happening in your body, emotions, and thoughts before reacting.

02

Regulate

Use grounding, breath, movement, or sensory practices to support your nervous system.

03

Reflect

Explore patterns, needs, boundaries, and emotional meaning with compassion.

04

Reconnect

Return to yourself, your values, your relationships, and your community.

05

Practice

Build small, repeatable habits that make emotional wellness part of daily life.
